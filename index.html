2017 Christmas Trip

My friends and I had discussed having a reunion many times in the past. Due to differences in our schedules, we couldn't come to a consensus on either a place or time. A few weeks ahead of the Christmas of last year, we brought it up again, made it work this time and decided to have the reunion in Europe. I'm inviting you here to browse some of the places my friends and I visited during our 2017 trip.

It started in London, from which we took the train to Paris.

Then, we drove from Paris to Luxembourg via Belgium, The Netherlands & Germany.

And, from Luxembourg back to Paris.

Then, the train from Paris back to London.
